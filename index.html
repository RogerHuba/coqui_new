Drink Selections

The bar serves a variety of traditional Puerto Rican cocktails such as the "Mojito Criollo" which is a mojito variation made with local rum and the use of local mint to add a local touch to it.


For those who prefer a different type of drink, The Coqui also offers a wide selection of rum, tequila, mezcal, whiskey, and gin.

Nightlife

Coqui is a bar that prides itself on being inclusive and celebrating the Puerto Rican culture. You can expect regular events like salsa nights and live music shows that reflect the island's culture and people. The bar provides a diverse and welcoming environment that makes everyone feel at home.
